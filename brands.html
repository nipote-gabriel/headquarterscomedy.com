Comedy for Brands

Live shows and events that people actually want to attend.

Headquarters Comedy produces live comedy shows for companies and brands. Whether it's a company offsite, a client event, a product launch, or a team night out — we make it funny, polished, and genuinely fun to be at.

What We Do

🎤

Hosted Comedy Shows

A fully produced live comedy show hosted by Gabe, featuring a curated lineup of professional comedians. We handle everything — venue coordination, lineup booking, hosting, and production.

🏢

Corporate Events

Comedy for company offsites, holiday parties, team-building events, and client entertainment. Clean, professional, and actually funny — not the kind of thing people groan about in the Slack channel the next day.

🚀

Launch Events

Make your product launch, campaign reveal, or brand moment memorable. We produce events that give people a real reason to show up, stay, and talk about it afterward.

🎯

Custom Programming

Have something specific in mind? We'll build it. Panel discussions, roasts, themed shows, hybrid formats — if you can describe it, we can make it happen.

📍

Venue Sourcing

We know the city's best comedy-ready spaces and can help identify the right venue for your audience size, vibe, and budget. Or we'll come to yours.

🤝

Sponsorships

Sponsor one of our public shows and get your brand in front of a smart, engaged audience that actually shows up to things. Tasteful, effective, not embarrassing.

How It Works

1

Tell Us What You Need

Send us an email with your event goals, audience, date range, and budget. The more detail the better.

2

We Put Together a Proposal

We'll come back with a format recommendation, lineup concept, and pricing. Usually within a few days.

3

We Handle the Rest

Once we're aligned, we take it from there. You show up, enjoy the show, take the credit.

Let's Make Something Great

Ready to put on a show your team or clients will actually remember? Drop us a line and we'll figure out the rest.

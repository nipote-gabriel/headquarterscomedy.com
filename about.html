About Headquarters Comedy

Our Mission

Headquarters Comedy is a comedy company built on the belief that laughter is serious business. We create, produce, and perform original comedy — stand-up, sketch, video, and live events — with a crew of people who are genuinely funny and completely unqualified for anything else. Our mission is simple: make something that makes people laugh, and do it often.

Our Approach

We don't take shortcuts. Every show, every video, every piece of content is built from scratch by comedians who care about the craft. We work with writers, performers, and producers who are obsessed with the thing that makes a joke actually land. The result is comedy that doesn't feel like content — it feels like something someone actually made.

Join Us

Subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on socials, and come to a show. We're based in NYC and we're out here making stuff regularly. The best way to stay in the loop is to actually be in the loop.

About

The person behind the company.

Gabe Nipote

Gabe Nipote is a comedian, writer, and producer based in New York City. He is the founder of Headquarters Comedy, where he produces live shows, writes and performs original material, and generally tries to make things funnier than they have any right to be.

What He Does

Gabe performs stand-up comedy regularly around NYC, produces and hosts the HQ Show, and writes across formats — stand-up, sketch, and whatever else seems worth trying. He also works with brands and companies to bring live comedy into their events. If you've seen a Headquarters Comedy show, you've probably seen him either on stage or scrambling behind the scenes to make it happen.

Get in Touch

For booking, brand partnerships, or general inquiries, reach out directly at gabe@headquarterscomedy.com. You can also find more about Gabe's work at gabenipote.com.

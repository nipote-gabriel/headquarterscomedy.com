About Headquarters Comedy

What is Headquarters?

Headquarters Comedy produces pop-up standup shows in NYC, featuring some of the most talented up and coming comics in the world. Our shows are known for being irreverent, smart, and riotous. Subscribe to our mailing list to get notified about shows. We also work with brands to help them create memorable events for their employees.

Who runs Headquarters?

Headquarters Comedy was started in 2026 by Gabe Nipote, a comedian & entrepreneur based in New York City. Prior to Headquarters, Gabe had a software company called Halla, which was acquired by Instacart in 2025.

Get in touch

For booking, brand partnerships, or general inquiries, reach out directly at gabe@headquarterscomedy.com or connect on socials. You can also subscribe to our mailing list here: headquarterscomedy.beehiiv.com.
